CONVIVIAL CITY

A workshop on the future of Eindhoven's Gasfabriek facilitated through the use of virtual collaging tools.

    How can values like conviviality be used to anchor the imagination of hopeful futures?

    ❮ ❯

    Overview
    On December 10th and 22nd of 2022, a dozen of Eindhoven residents got together at Stadslab to ask themselves: What does it take for a neighborhood to be convivial?

    Using the NRE neighborhood as their starting point where the (Stadslab is located), they imagined three scenarios exploring how the neighborhood might have to adapt to remain a “convivial” place in the face of the city’s transformation.

    The NRE offers a unique location and community to explore the question, located on the edge of the city center, between the canal and the river Dommel, a unique piece of Eindhoven opens up. Adventurous residents and creative entrepreneurs are building this place together.

    Old industry is making way for new energy.
