# How can imagining the future of a single contested site like Smedjegatan 5, help reflect on the tensions shaping the city of Gothenburg?




## Overview

|                           |                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         |
| ------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| ![[DiscoPerformance.gif]] | An audiovisual performance presented live at Smedjegatan 5 in Gothenburg aka [Syntropia](https://www.syntropia.space/). The site-specific work questions the past, present, and future of this address by putting it in dialogue with a spiritual twin, the [Whirling-In-Rags](https://discoelysium.fandom.com/wiki/Whirling-in-Rags).<br><br>This performance uses [Streaming Diffusion](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=X4rlC6y1ahw) and Touchdesigner to reinterpret archival images, video game footage from [Disco Elysium](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Disco_Elysium), and footage of the area I captured during neighborhood walks. The piece was performed using live generated visuals accompanied by a text I wrote and performed by Oshin Siao Bhatt, on the potential futures of the area.<br><br>Photos of event by Robin Rydén.<br><br> |
