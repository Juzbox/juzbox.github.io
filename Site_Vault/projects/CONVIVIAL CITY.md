| ![[team001.gif]] | On December 10th and 22nd of 2022, a dozen of Eindhoven residents got together at Stadslab to ask themselves: What does it take for a neighborhood to be convivial?<br><br>Using the NRE neighborhood as their starting point where the (Stadslab is located), they imagined three scenarios exploring how the neighborhood might have to adapt to remain a “convivial” place in the face of the city’s transformation.<br><br>The NRE offers a unique location and community to explore the question, located on the edge of the city center, between the canal and the river Dommel, a unique piece of Eindhoven opens up. Adventurous residents and creative entrepreneurs are building this place together.<br><br>Old industry is making way for new energy.<br><br> |
