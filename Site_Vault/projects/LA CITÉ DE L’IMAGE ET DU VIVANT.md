# How must image-making crafts and their education be reimagined in light of recently emerging digital possibilities? 


## Overview

| **BRIEF**                                                                                                                                              | **APPROACH**                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            |
| ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Create guidelines and case studies to inform the programming of a cutting-edge campus dedicated to digital image production, in Arles, France.<br><br> | For this project I worked with Fabbula on two key deliverables:<br>- The outlining of the educational programs to be offered on this campus which sought to embrace the increasingly multi-disciplinary nature of digital image making.<br>- The production of a virtual exhibit in which visitors could see how the program was designed and attend virtual brainstorming<br>sessions. |

## Program design

The vision was developed to reflect the increasingly porous nature of creative image-making through digital mediums. The program was informed by the close study of complementary sites, their educational offerings, and comparing them to Arles' specific context as a thriving cultural hub.
